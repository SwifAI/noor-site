Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my location?

Open Settings (gear icon) and tap "Change Location" to select a new city or use your current location.

How do I change the calculation method?

Open Prayer Times, scroll to the bottom, and tap the calculation method or madhab to change it.

Prayer times seem incorrect

Make sure you have the correct calculation method selected for your region. Different methods can produce slightly different times.

The Qibla compass isn't working

Ensure you've granted location access. Try calibrating your compass by moving your phone in a figure-8 motion. The simulator does not support compass.

Does Noor collect my data?

No. Noor has no analytics, no tracking, no ads, and no servers. Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.
