Privacy Policy

Effective: February 14, 2026

Noor ("the App") is developed by Forsan. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Noor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server. We have no analytics, no tracking, no ads, and no user accounts.

Location

The App requests location access solely to calculate accurate prayer times for your city. Your location is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to any external server. You may also choose a city manually without granting location access.

Local Storage

The App stores your preferences (selected city, calculation method, fasting streak) locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

StoreKit

If you choose to leave a tip via the App, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

Noor does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since no data is collected from any user, the App is safe for all ages.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at forsan@toneminder.app.
